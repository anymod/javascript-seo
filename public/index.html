10 ways to get content onto a page

There are 10 ways to get content onto a page (shown below as A-J). This page has all 10.

As each JS option executes on this page, its letter is added to this page's <h1> tag above, to the <title> and <meta> tags in the document's <head>, and to the table and image below.

If a letter shows in a given search engine's results, it means that method was indexed.
